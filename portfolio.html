Rock Paper Scissors

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a quick hand game played between two people. Each player chooses one of three shapes — rock (fist), paper (flat hand), or scissors (two fingers). Rock beats scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. If both choose the same, it’s a tie. It’s a simple, fun way to make fair decisions or settle small disputes.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player game played on a 3×3 grid. Players take turns marking X or O in empty squares, aiming to get three of their symbols in a row—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. It’s a simple yet strategic game of logic, quick thinking, and anticipation that’s easy to learn and fun for all ages.
